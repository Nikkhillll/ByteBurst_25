About Us

The Coders and Developers Club is a community of passionate students who love to code, build, and innovate. Whether you're into web development, app creation, AI, or open source — there's a place for you here.

  • Weekly coding sessions, hackathons, and workshops
  • Real-world projects, mentorship, and portfolio building
  • Teamwork, leadership, and career development opportunities

Join us to grow as a developer, contribute to exciting tech, and collaborate with like-minded peers.
