About Our Board

The Editorial Board, the official publishing body of Madan Mohan Malaviya University of Technology, holds a legacy of more than half a century and stands as a paragon of literary excellence, orchestrating a symphony of words and wisdom.

The Editorial Board organizes illustrious roster of events - Vagmita, Arunoday, Annual Debate Competition, Twist and Twain, Spell Czar, Annual Photoshoot and vibrant quizzes, essays, and poetry sessions that form the crucible where intellect and artistry meld. Under the umbrella of Cultural Sub-Council, The Editorial Board also organizes the University's grandiose Art, Cultural, and Literary fest, Abhyudaya.

The Editorial Board crafts an array of esteemed publications. The erudite monthly newsletter, Tiresia and the venerable Annual University Magazine, Malvika, stand as testaments to the University's pulsating academic life and cultural ethos.
